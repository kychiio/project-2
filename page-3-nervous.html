NERVOUS SYSTEM
________________

coordinates the actions and senses of the body by sending electrical messages to and from the brain. the basic unit of the nervous system is a nerve cell, or neuron. the human brain contains about 100 billion neurons. a neuron has a cell body, which includes the cell nucleus, and special extensions called axons and dendrites. bundles of axons, called nerves, are found throughout the body. axons and dendrites allow neurons to communicate, even across long distances.
